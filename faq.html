Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our website analysis service.

What is AIFR?

AIFR (Artificial Intelligence Framework Rating) is our scoring system that rates websites from 0-100 based on design, mobile readiness, content, contact info, SEO, and images. It's designed to give you a clear picture of your website's overall quality.

How much does it cost?

The basic website analysis is FREE! You can enter any website URL and get an instant AIFR score. For detailed reports with recommendations and ongoing monitoring, please contact us for a quote.

How long does an analysis take?

Our automated analysis takes only a few seconds to give you an instant AIFR score. You'll see the results immediately after clicking "Analyze".

Can you analyze any website?

Yes! We can analyze most websites. Just enter the URL in our analyzer and get instant results. Some websites with special protections may not be accessible, but we'll let you know if that happens.

How do I get a full report?

Contact us through the contact form and we'll provide you with a detailed analysis and personalized recommendations to improve your website.

What does the AIFR score mean?

The AIFR score ranges from 0-100. 80+ is excellent, 60-79 is good, 40-59 is moderate, and below 40 needs attention. Each category (design, mobile, content, contact, SEO, images) is scored separately.

Do you offer website improvement services?

Yes! After the analysis, we can provide recommendations and even help implement improvements. Contact us to discuss your needs.

How accurate is the analysis?

Our analysis is automated and provides a good baseline assessment. For a more comprehensive review, we offer manual analysis services. Contact us for details.
