What is AIFR and how does it work?

AIFR (Artificial Intelligence Framework Rating) is our scoring system that rates websites from 0-100. We analyze your site across 5 key areas: Design (look and feel), SEO (Google visibility), Content (text quality), Images (optimization), and Mobile (phone/tablet friendliness). A score of 70+ means your site is performing well.
